engaging the wider community

Through the NumFOCUS organisation, equadratures participates in the Google Summer of Code. This programme seeks to involve undergraduate students in the development of open-source tools. Individual equadratures’ projects are designed to empower students with useful mathematical and statistical machinery, while helping the broader user community. Students are mentored by our development team and encouraged at each step of the GSOC programme.

Our past students are listed below.
  • 2020 Barney Hill
    Polynomial regression trees with orthogonal polynomials.
  • 2021 Simardeep Singh Sethi
    Developing an app for uncertainty quantification using polynomial chaos methods.
